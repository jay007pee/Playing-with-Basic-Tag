Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Founder & CEO of ED Rose Designs James Pritchett spent 22 years serving our country in the Indiana Army National Guard. He has always wanted to run and own his own business. He started a small community business making custom T-Shirts in the great state of Indiana in October of 2020. He created a LLC and named the company after his late Mother. His goal is to brand his own apparel. Business went well creating birthday, funeral, and personal shirts, but he has fallen in love with designing. James knowing GOD has something more for him, he wants everything that GOD has in store for him. He stepping out on faith. He being a man of GOD and a man of our country, the great U.S.A.
